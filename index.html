About Me

I am a final-year BS-MS Mathematics student at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, with a dedicated research focus in Theoretical Computer Science. My primary interests lie at the intersection of cryptography, complexity theory, and algorithms. I am particularly passionate about post-quantum cryptography and the design of secure and efficient protocols for real-world applications. My goal is to pursue a PhD to contribute to the foundational security of our future digital infrastructure.
